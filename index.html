About Me

I am a third-year B.Tech student at VIT Chennai specializing in AI and Machine Learning, with a strong focus on building and understanding data-driven systems.

My work revolves around areas like natural language processing and computer vision, using tools such as Python and TensorFlow to design and experiment with models that learn patterns in data. I’m particularly interested in how abstract mathematical concepts translate into real-world intelligent behavior.

Beyond coursework, I actively contribute to university club activities, collaborating on technical and creative initiatives in team environments. I’ve also co-authored a peer-reviewed book chapter on AI-driven educational assessment, published by Taylor & Francis and indexed in Scopus.

Alongside this, I write long-form fiction, including my published novel 'Moon of Yesterday', as a way to explore ideas at the intersection of technology and human expression.
