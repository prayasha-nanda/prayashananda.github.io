Academic & Literary Contributions

Transforming Education Through AI-Powered Personalized Assessment

Chapter 7 of the volume "Adopting Artificial Intelligence Tools in Higher Education – Student Assessment"

It explores how AI can support the development of personalized, responsive, and learner-centered assessment systems.
My work in this chapter focused on adapting AI-driven assessment methodologies to the Indian education system. It challenged me to consider the complexities of local contexts, giving me a broader appreciation for the potential and limitations of AI in education.

Elsewhere in August

Contributor to the poetry anthology Elsewhere in August, featuring my tribute poem to the famous artist wowaka, who changed the entire music industry forever with his high bpm (beats per minute) songs and lyrics that did not shy away from the harsh reality, using Vocaloid software.

Technical Writing

Regular contributor to the official technical blog for my college club, covering topics ranging from Digital Ownership to evolving landscape of Artificial Intelligence.

About Prayasha

Prayasha Nanda is a student and writer, exploring stories at the intersection of technology and human emotion. Her work spans soft sci-fi and poetry, often centered on memory, time, and the quiet weight of things left unsaid.

Currently pursuing Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization in AIML, she is interested in building and understanding intelligent systems, and how technology shapes the way we interact with the world.
